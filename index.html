What is Meditation?

Meditation is a practice where an individual uses a technique – such as mindfulness, or focusing the mind on a particular object, thought, or activity – to train attention and awareness, and achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm and stable state.

Benefits of Meditation

  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Increases self-awareness
  • Improves focus and concentration
  • Promotes emotional well-being

Types of Meditation

Mindfulness Meditation

Mindfulness meditation involves paying attention to the present moment and observing thoughts and sensations without judgment. It can be practiced while sitting, standing, walking, or doing other activities.

Transcendental Meditation

Transcendental meditation is a simple, natural technique that involves sitting with eyes closed and repeating a mantra silently. It is practiced for 20 minutes twice a day.

Loving-Kindness Meditation

Loving-kindness meditation involves sending love and positive energy to oneself and others. It can help to reduce negative feelings and promote feelings of well-being.
